Learn More About Our Programs

Our Mission & Vision

The Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade & Investment (FMITI) is focused on strengthening Nigeria’s economy by driving industrial growth, facilitating trade, and attracting investment. We collaborate across sectors to support local businesses, enhance industrial capacity, and integrate Nigeria into global markets. Our goal is to create a sustainable and inclusive economy through strategic policies, impactful initiatives, and productive partnerships.

Key Functions and Responsibilities of the Ministry

Policy Development and Implementation

Developing strategic policies that drive industrial growth, support entrepreneurship, and attract foreign investments.

Trade Promotion and Facilitation

Supporting Nigerian businesses in accessing local and global markets through effective trade strategies.

Investment Promotion

Creating an investor-friendly environment through incentives, resources, and services that attract and retain investment.

SME Empowerment

Providing resources, grants, and training to small and medium enterprises (SMEs), empowering them to succeed.

Consumer Protection and Regulation

Ensuring fair practices and standards in industry and trade, safeguarding the interests of Nigerian consumers.

Data and Industry Analysis

Providing insights and analysis to inform policies and drive evidence-based decision-making for economic growth.

The Benefits of Our Initiatives for Businesses and the Public

The Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade & Investment (FMITI) is focused on strengthening Nigeria’s economy by driving industrial growth, facilitating trade, and attracting investment. We collaborate across sectors to support local businesses, enhance industrial capacity, and integrate Nigeria into global markets. Our goal is to create a sustainable and inclusive economy through strategic policies, impactful initiatives, and productive partnerships.

For Nigerian Businesses

Access to comprehensive support services, funding opportunities, market insights, and training to strengthen their competitive edge locally and globally.

For Foreign Investors

A reliable partner in understanding Nigeria’s potential, supported by policy incentives, investment protections, and robust market opportunities.

For the National Economy

By driving industrial growth, diversifying sources of national income, and strengthening Nigeria's position in global markets, our initiatives foster long-term economic stability and resilience.

For the Nigerian Public

Our initiatives lead to sustainable job creation, improved skill development, and greater access to essential goods and services, contributing to a higher standard of living across the nation.

How We Operate to Support Growth and Prosperity

Through a collaborative, transparent, and process-driven approach, the Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade & Investment ensures that businesses and investors can navigate regulations, access resources, and build successful partnerships. Here’s how we work:

01

Strategic Policy Formulation

We create policies that drive industrial growth, trade, and investment, ensuring a stable environment for local and foreign businesses.

02

Program Implementation

Launching targeted initiatives, grants, and training programs that stimulate growth, support SMEs, and drive trade.

03

Business and Investor Support

Providing resources and services to guide businesses and investors, from licensing to market access and beyond.

04

Regulatory Oversight

Ensuring compliance with standards to protect consumers and maintain a fair market environment.

05

Data and Insights

Conducting research, gathering data, and offering insights on industry and trade trends to inform decision-making.

Our Mission: Fostering Growth, Empowering Trade, and Attracting Investment

The Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade & Investment is dedicated to strengthening Nigeria’s economy by driving industrial growth, enhancing trade relations, and attracting both domestic and international investment. We aim to create a conducive environment for businesses to thrive, leveraging strategic policies, innovative initiatives, and sustainable practices. Our focus is to support local industries, foster global competitiveness, and ensure that Nigeria becomes a preferred destination for investment.
